## Remaining Strengthening Areas

The release-blocking missing invariants from the first audit are closed. The remaining non-zero gaps are narrower:

1. adapter retryable and transport-failure matrices are still thinner than the semantic success and incompatibility coverage
2. deterministic scope membership and broader provenance composition would benefit from a richer repeated-evaluation corpus
3. thin-adapter, thin-RN, and client-boundary architectural invariants are mapped today, but they are still supported more by black-box behavior and corpus usage than by dedicated structure tests
